About My Homes

Discover the story behind Kenya's most trusted real estate platform

Our Story

Founded in 2010, My Homes began as a small real estate agency in Nairobi with a vision to transform the property market in Kenya. What started as a modest office with just three employees has grown into the country's leading digital real estate platform.

Our journey has been driven by a passion for connecting people with their dream homes while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism in the industry.

Today, we serve thousands of clients across all major Kenyan cities, helping them navigate the property market with confidence and ease.

Our Mission

At My Homes, we believe everyone deserves a place they can call home. Our mission is to simplify the process of buying, selling, and renting properties while ensuring transparency at every step.

We're committed to:

- Providing accurate, up-to-date property information

- Offering expert guidance through every transaction

- Developing innovative tools to empower our clients

- Building long-term relationships based on trust

Our Values

Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency, ensuring our clients can trust every piece of advice and every property listing.

Excellence: From our website experience to our customer service, we strive for the highest standards in everything we do.

Innovation: We continuously evolve our platform to incorporate the latest technologies that benefit our users.

Community: We believe in giving back and actively participate in housing initiatives that benefit Kenyan communities.
