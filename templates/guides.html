Buying a Property: Step-by-Step Guide

1

Determine Your Budget

Before searching, calculate how much you can afford. Consider:

  • Down payment (typically 10-20%)
  • Mortgage pre-approval
  • Closing costs (2-5% of property value)

A well-planned budget ensures a smooth buying process.

2

Find the Right Property

Work with a real estate agent or browse listings online. Key factors:

  • Location (schools, transport, safety)
  • Property condition (inspection needed)
  • Future resale value

Choose a property that fits your lifestyle.

3

Make an Offer & Negotiate

Once you find a property:

  • Submit a formal offer
  • Negotiate price and terms
  • Sign a purchase agreement

A well-negotiated deal saves money.

4

Close the Deal

Final steps:

  • Final mortgage approval
  • Home inspection
  • Sign legal documents
  • Receive keys!

Selling a Property: Step-by-Step Guide

1

Prepare Your Home

Enhance appeal before listing:

  • Declutter & deep clean
  • Minor repairs (paint, fixtures)
  • Professional staging (optional)

A well-staged home attracts buyers.

2

Set the Right Price

Factors to consider:

  • Comparative market analysis (CMA)
  • Local market trends
  • Property condition

Pricing competitively ensures faster sales.

3

Market Your Property

Effective strategies:

  • High-quality photos & virtual tours
  • List on multiple platforms
  • Open houses & private showings

Good marketing attracts serious buyers.

4

Close the Sale

Final steps:

  • Review offers
  • Negotiate terms
  • Sign contracts
  • Transfer ownership
