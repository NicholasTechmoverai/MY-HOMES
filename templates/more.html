Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine the right listing price for my home?

We recommend a comparative market analysis (CMA) which evaluates similar recently sold properties in your area, current market trends, and your home's unique features. Our agents can provide a free, no-obligation valuation.

What are the benefits of renting vs. buying?

Renting offers flexibility and fewer maintenance responsibilities, while buying builds equity and offers tax benefits. The right choice depends on your financial situation, lifestyle, and long-term goals. Use our "Rent vs. Buy Calculator" to compare costs.

How can I improve my credit score before applying for a mortgage?

Key steps include: paying bills on time, reducing credit card balances, avoiding new credit applications, and checking your credit report for errors. Most lenders recommend a score of at least 620 for conventional loans, with better rates available for scores above 740.
